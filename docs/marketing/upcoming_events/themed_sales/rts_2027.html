Eligibility

RTS games are more than the sum of its parts—eligible games will at minimum be played in real-time and be under the strategy umbrella, but have additional components that will make it recognizable as an "RTS" title.

Eligible titles will likely include the following:
  • The game is played in real-time (AFKing means losing)
  • The game involves high levels of strategic play, including build / research planning, anticipating enemy movements, and resource management
  • The player controls multiple units, and can manage them as a collective squad or a single unit
  • Combat is the primary gameplay loop, typically done in a squad vs. squad format as opposed to unit vs. unit
  • Typically played with keyboard/mouse for precise unit control
  • A lose condition
  • Some amount of base building, typically centered around unit creation
  • An open field / battlemap where players fight over resources and neutral space

If your game falls into the following genres, it is likely ineligible for RTS Fest:
  • Tower Defense
  • Shooting (some RTS titles will feature shooting as a gameplay element, but if you're a shooter first and an RTS second, you're likely ineligible)
  • God Games
  • City Builders
  • 4X or Grand Strategy

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I haven't received an email invitation and have a game I believe fits the eligibility criteria described above. How can I confirm whether my game has been invited and request an invitation if it has not been invited?
A. If you haven't received an email invitation and believe you have a game that fits the eligibility criteria described above, please check:

  • Your list of eligible games here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/rts_2027
  • The registration page for your game by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/rts_2027/
  • Your base game's App Admin page (in the Marketing Tools & Data section) for the opt-in by appending your appID here: https://partner.steamgames.com/apps/landing/

If your game is not found via the paths above and you believe it meets the eligibility criteria, please use the appeals process available at the bottom of the registration page.

If your game was found via the paths above but you didn't receive an email, please check your Steamworks communication preferences (instructions available in the Steam Themed Sale Events parent documentation page).

Q. Do I need to register my game before entering a discount?
A. Yes, you need to do the following things in order:
  1. Follow the link in the invitation email, or click this link to see your eligible games and register each: https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/rts_2027
  2. Once a game is registered, you can then enter discounts on the Discount Management page: https://partner.steamgames.com/promotion/discounts/dashboard/?16755

Q. Will there be livestreaming as part of this event?
A. Livestreaming will not be featured on the sale page, but you're welcome to livestream on your own game's store page as you wish.

Q. I've changed my plans. How do I cancel my registration?
A. Just return to the registration page for your game by inserting your appID at the end of this URL https://partner.steamgames.com/optin/sale/rts_2027/<appid> and deselect the checkbox at the very bottom of the page to remove your game from the event.

Q. Can I promote our participation in the event on our own channels before the trailer goes live?
A. Technically yes; you can publish once the Asset Kit is available for download, but we recommend waiting until the event trailer is live (again, roughly 4 days prior to the event) as a more effective communications beat with your audience.
